Andy is a photographer based in Seoul, Korea. While he was in London for study abroad, he found the interest in photography. His love for photography began with landscape, urban views, and amazing scenery of Europe. He first was into more landscape photography, and as he got back to Korea his main focus moved on to portraits and snapshots. Being surrounded by amazing people, it wasn't hard to capture fascinating moments and notice the effortless beauty of everyday life.

After working with a couple of professional models, now he is trying to take photography more seriously and take it to the next level. Working with Pierre Arden, he's now capable of doing product photography as well.

From then on Andy began working with a youtube team cover photographing team, he is getting more experienced in the field. He has some more to improve, but Andy now prides himself as in creating/capturing moments for individuals to help enhance their business, happiness, and social media presence
